About Us

Inspired by the Splendour of Creation, Guided by Eternal Light

Splendour Art is an interior art studio founded by artist Anastasia Temkina.

At the heart of our work lies the belief that beauty can carry spirit — that form, texture, and light can become vessels for meaning.

Each piece is created with an intuitive attention to that delicate threshold where the physical meets the eternal, and where materials begin to speak without words.

Our studio creates bespoke decorative artworks — from classical paintings and textured wall panels to intricate, one-of-a-kind compositions in advanced mixed media.

We work with plaster, mirrors, gold leaf, glass painting, glaze, semi-precious stones, Swarovski crystals, and cubic zirconia, combining these elements to craft pieces that exist as single originals — never repeated.

We produce works of any size, from intimate accents to large-scale panels that replicate the natural splendour of stone slices, tailored to complement both private and public interiors.

Every commissioned artwork is developed in close dialogue with the client. Together, we select tones, finishes, and materials, ensuring that the final piece resonates not only with the space, but with the person who will live alongside it.

One of our studio's distinct features is the creation of art suitable for humid Asian climates. A protective glazed finish allows the artworks to be placed in moist environments — such as bathrooms or spa areas — and remain pristine for decades.
